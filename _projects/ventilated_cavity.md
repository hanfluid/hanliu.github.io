We perform high-fidelity numerical simulations to investigate the flow around a ventilated cavity at low Reynolds numbers. The study provides fundamental insights into the cavity dynamics, wake structure, and the interaction between the ventilation gas and the surrounding flow.

**Publication:**

- Liu, H., Xiao, Z. & Shen, L. (2023), "Simulation-based study of low Reynolds number flow around a ventilated cavity," _Journal of Fluid Mechanics_, Vol. 966, A20.
