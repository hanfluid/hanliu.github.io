We use large-eddy simulation (LES) to investigate the performance of drag-type vertical axis hydrokinetic turbines in open channel flows, developing a co-design strategy for optimizing turbine geometry and placement for renewable energy applications.

**Publication:**

- Gao, J., Liu, H., Lee, J., Zheng, Y., Guala, M. & Shen, L. (2021), "Large-eddy simulation and co-design strategy for a drag-type vertical axis hydrokinetic turbine in open channel flows," _Renewable Energy_.
