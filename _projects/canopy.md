We perform simulation-based study of turbulent aquatic canopy flows with flexible stems, coupling fluid dynamics with structural mechanics to capture the two-way interaction between turbulent flow and flexible vegetation.

**Publication:**

- He, S., Liu, H. & Shen, L. (2022), "Simulation-based study of turbulent aquatic canopy flows with flexible stems," _Journal of Fluid Mechanics_, Vol. 947, A33.
