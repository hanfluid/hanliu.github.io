We study the preferential concentration of heavy particles in compressible isotropic turbulence. The simulations reveal how particles with different Stokes numbers (St = 0, 0.1, 1, 5) exhibit distinct clustering behaviors due to the interplay between particle inertia and turbulent flow structures.

**Publication:**

- Zhang, Q., Liu, H., Ma, Z. & Xiao, Z. (2016), "Preferential concentration of heavy particles in compressible isotropic turbulence," _Physics of Fluids_, 28, 055104.
